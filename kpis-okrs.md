## KPIs (Key Performance Indicators)
KPIs are metrics used to measure performance and track how well a process or team is operating.

### Examples
- Task completion rate
- Response time between teams
- Event execution without issues
- Budget adherence

## OKRs (Objectives and Key Results)
OKRs define goals and measurable outcomes.

### Example
**Objective:** Improve team coordination  
**Key Results:**
- Reduce response time by 30%
- Document core processes
- Hold bi-weekly sync meetings

KPIs help measure performance, while OKRs provide direction and focus.
